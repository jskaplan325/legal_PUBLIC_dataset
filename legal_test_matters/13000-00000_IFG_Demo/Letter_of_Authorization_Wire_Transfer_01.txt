SECTION 4 - NON-STANDING WIRE INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For wire transfers to accounts NOT listed in Section 3, the following procedures
shall apply:

A. WRITTEN AUTHORIZATION REQUIRED
   All wire transfers to non-standing accounts require prior written authorization
   signed by two (2) authorized signatories.

B. MINIMUM NOTICE PERIOD
   Written authorization must be received by the Custodian at least two (2)
   business days prior to the requested transfer date for amounts up to
   $10,000,000, and at least three (3) business days for amounts exceeding
   $10,000,000.

C. VERIFICATION PROCEDURES
   For transfers exceeding $1,000,000, the Custodian shall conduct callback
   verification to the designated callback numbers listed in Section 6.

D. REQUIRED INFORMATION
   Authorization for non-standing transfers must include:
   - Beneficiary bank name and address
   - ABA routing number and/or SWIFT code
   - Beneficiary account name and number
   - Transfer amount
   - Value date
   - Purpose/reference information
   - Signatures of two authorized signatories
