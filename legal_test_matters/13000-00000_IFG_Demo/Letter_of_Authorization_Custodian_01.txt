SCOPE OF AUTHORIZATION

The Account Holder hereby authorizes the Custodian to:

1. SECURITIES TRANSACTIONS
   (a) Accept and execute instructions for the purchase, sale, exchange, conversion,
       or transfer of securities held in or to be deposited into the account;
   (b) Deliver securities against payment or free of payment as instructed;
   (c) Receive securities against payment or free of payment as instructed;
   (d) Execute foreign exchange transactions as necessary to settle securities
       transactions.

2. CASH MOVEMENTS
   (a) Wire funds to designated accounts as instructed in accordance with the
       Standing Wire Instructions attached hereto as Exhibit A;
   (b) Process capital calls from the Fund;
   (c) Process distributions to limited partners as instructed by the Administrator;
   (d) Pay fees, expenses, and other obligations of the Fund as directed.

3. ACCOUNT INFORMATION
   (a) Provide account statements, transaction confirmations, and other account
       information to the authorized parties;
   (b) Respond to inquiries from authorized parties regarding account status,
       positions, and transactions;
   (c) Provide tax reporting information as required.

4. CORPORATE ACTIONS
   (a) Process corporate actions (dividends, rights offerings, tender offers,
       mergers, etc.) in accordance with instructions from authorized parties;
   (b) In the absence of instructions, take no action or follow standing
       instructions previously provided in writing.

TERM AND TERMINATION

This Letter of Authorization shall remain in full force and effect until:

(a) Revoked in writing by the Account Holder with at least thirty (30) days'
    prior written notice to the Custodian;

(b) Terminated by the Custodian with at least sixty (60) days' prior written
    notice to the Account Holder;

(c) The Account Holder's account with the Custodian is closed; or

(d) Superseded by a subsequent Letter of Authorization executed by the Account Holder.

Any revocation or termination shall not affect any transactions initiated prior to
the effective date of such revocation or termination.
