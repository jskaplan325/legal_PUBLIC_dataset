RECITALS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

WHEREAS, Meridian Growth Fund I, LP (the "Fund") has engaged Citco Fund Services
(USA) Inc. (the "Administrator") to provide fund administration services pursuant
to that certain Fund Administration Agreement dated August 1, 2023 (the
"Administration Agreement");

WHEREAS, the Fund maintains custodial accounts with Northern Trust Corporation
(the "Custodian") for the safekeeping of Fund assets;

WHEREAS, in order for the Administrator to fulfill its duties under the
Administration Agreement, the Administrator requires access to certain account
information and the authority to provide instructions to the Custodian on
behalf of the Fund;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Fund and the General Partner hereby authorize and instruct
the Custodian as follows:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 - AUTHORIZATION SCOPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Custodian is hereby authorized to:

1.1 INFORMATION ACCESS

    (a) Provide the Administrator with access to all account information,
        including but not limited to:
        - Daily position reports
        - Transaction confirmations
        - Cash balances and movements
        - Securities holdings and valuations
        - Corporate action notifications
        - Tax lot information
        - Cost basis reports
        - Monthly and quarterly statements

    (b) Grant the Administrator online access to the Custodian's client portal
        (Northern Trust Passport or successor system) with view-only access to
        account information.

    (c) Respond to inquiries from the Administrator regarding account status,
        positions, pending transactions, and related matters.

1.2 INSTRUCTION AUTHORITY

    Subject to the limitations set forth in Section 2, the Custodian is
    authorized to accept and act upon the following types of instructions
    from the Administrator:

    (a) SETTLEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
        - Delivery versus payment (DVP) and receive versus payment (RVP)
          settlement instructions for securities transactions
        - Free delivery and free receipt instructions for securities transfers
        - Foreign exchange settlement instructions

    (b) CASH MANAGEMENT
        - Instructions to move cash between Fund accounts
        - Instructions to invest idle cash balances in approved money market
          funds or short-term instruments
        - Instructions to fund capital calls to/from the Fund's capital
          call account

    (c) CORPORATE ACTIONS
        - Election instructions for voluntary corporate actions (tender
          offers, rights offerings, conversions, etc.)
        - Proxy voting instructions (if delegated to Administrator)

    (d) REPORTING
        - Requests for account statements, reports, and confirmations
        - Requests for tax reporting information
        - Requests for audit confirmations

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 - LIMITATIONS ON AUTHORITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Administrator's authority under this Letter of Authorization is subject to
the following limitations:

2.1 PROHIBITED ACTIONS

    The Administrator is NOT authorized to:

    (a) Initiate wire transfers to accounts not pre-approved in the Fund's
        standing wire instructions on file with the Custodian;

    (b) Add, modify, or delete standing wire instructions;

    (c) Add, modify, or delete authorized signatories;

    (d) Close or open accounts;

    (e) Pledge, hypothecate, or encumber Fund assets;

    (f) Execute trades or investment decisions (which are the responsibility
        of the Investment Manager);

    (g) Take any action that would result in an overdraft of Fund accounts
        unless previously approved in writing by the General Partner.

2.2 MONETARY LIMITS

    The Administrator's authority to instruct cash movements is limited as
    follows:

    (a) Intra-Fund Transfers (between Fund accounts):
        - Daily limit: $25,000,000
        - Single transaction limit: $10,000,000

    (b) External Transfers (to standing instruction accounts only):
        - Daily limit: $15,000,000
        - Single transaction limit: $5,000,000

    Transfers exceeding these limits require written authorization from the
    General Partner.

2.3 DUAL AUTHORIZATION REQUIREMENTS

    The following actions require authorization from both the Administrator
    and the General Partner (or its designee):

    (a) Settlement of securities transactions exceeding $10,000,000;
    (b) Cash transfers exceeding the limits in Section 2.2;
    (c) Corporate action elections with material financial impact;
    (d) Any instruction that deviates from established procedures.

SECTION 5 - VERIFICATION PROCEDURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 CALLBACK VERIFICATION

    The Custodian shall conduct callback verification for:
    - All telephone instructions
    - Written instructions for amounts exceeding $1,000,000
    - Any instruction that appears unusual or inconsistent with normal patterns

    Callback shall be made to the phone numbers listed in Section 3 for the
    relevant authorized person.

5.2 SECONDARY VERIFICATION

    For instructions exceeding $5,000,000, the Custodian shall obtain secondary
    verification from the General Partner using the following contacts:

    Primary:   Katherine L. Wong, CFO
               Phone: (212) 555-4891

    Secondary: Michael S. Brennan, COO
               Phone: (212) 555-4892

5.3 DISCREPANCY RESOLUTION

    In the event of any discrepancy between instructions received from the
    Administrator and the General Partner, the Custodian shall:
    (a) Immediately notify both parties;
    (b) Take no action until the discrepancy is resolved; and
    (c) Follow the instructions of the General Partner in the event of
        irreconcilable conflict.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 - REPORTING AND NOTIFICATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6.1 ADMINISTRATOR REPORTING

    The Custodian shall provide the Administrator with:

    (a) Daily Reports:
        - Opening and closing cash balances
        - Pending settlements
        - Failed trades
        - Transaction confirmations

    (b) Weekly Reports:
        - Full portfolio holdings
        - Cash projections
        - Corporate action calendar

    (c) Monthly Reports:
        - Complete account statements
        - Performance attribution data
        - Reconciliation support

    (d) As-Requested:
        - Audit confirmations
        - Tax reporting information
        - Ad hoc reports

6.2 NOTIFICATIONS

    The Custodian shall notify the Administrator of:
    - Corporate actions (within 24 hours of receipt)
    - Failed settlements (same day)
    - Overdrafts or insufficient funds (immediately)
    - System outages affecting account access
    - Changes to Custodian procedures affecting the Fund

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 - DATA SECURITY AND CONFIDENTIALITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7.1 CONFIDENTIALITY

    The Administrator acknowledges that all Fund information received from
    the Custodian is confidential and agrees to:
    - Maintain such information in strict confidence;
    - Use such information solely for Fund administration purposes;
    - Not disclose such information to third parties except as required
      by law or authorized by the Fund.

7.2 DATA SECURITY

    The Administrator agrees to implement appropriate data security measures,
    including but not limited to:
    - Encryption of data in transit and at rest;
    - Access controls limiting information to authorized personnel;
    - Regular security assessments and audits;
    - Prompt notification of any security incidents.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 - INDEMNIFICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8.1 FUND INDEMNIFICATION

    The Fund and the General Partner jointly and severally agree to indemnify
    and hold harmless the Custodian from and against any and all claims,
    losses, liabilities, damages, and expenses arising out of or relating to:

    (a) The Custodian's good faith reliance upon instructions from the
        Administrator that the Custodian reasonably believes to be genuine
        and within the scope of the Administrator's authority hereunder;

    (b) Any action taken or omitted by the Custodian in accordance with
        this Letter of Authorization, except to the extent resulting from
        the Custodian's gross negligence, willful misconduct, or fraud.

8.2 ADMINISTRATOR INDEMNIFICATION

    The Administrator agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Fund and the
    Custodian from and against any claims, losses, liabilities, damages, and
    expenses arising out of:

    (a) Instructions provided by the Administrator that exceed the scope of
        authority granted herein;

    (b) Fraud, gross negligence, or willful misconduct by the Administrator
        or its personnel;

    (c) Breach of the confidentiality or data security provisions hereof.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 - TERM AND TERMINATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9.1 TERM

    This Letter of Authorization shall be effective upon execution and
    acceptance by all parties and shall remain in effect until terminated
    in accordance with Section 9.2.

9.2 TERMINATION

    This Letter of Authorization may be terminated:

    (a) By the Fund or General Partner, upon thirty (30) days' written
        notice to the Administrator and the Custodian;

    (b) By the Administrator, upon sixty (60) days' written notice to
        the Fund and the Custodian;

    (c) Automatically upon termination of the Administration Agreement;

    (d) Immediately upon written notice by any party in the event of a
        material breach by another party.

9.3 EFFECT OF TERMINATION

    Upon termination, the Administrator's authority to provide instructions
    to the Custodian shall cease, and the Custodian shall revoke the
    Administrator's access to account information and systems.
