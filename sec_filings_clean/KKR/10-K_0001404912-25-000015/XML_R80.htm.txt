- DefinitionConsists of realized gains and losses and unrealized gains and losses arising from investment activities. The majority of net gains (losses) from investment activities are related to private equity investments. Changes in net gains (losses) from investment activities between reporting periods is driven primarily by changes in the fair value of the investment portfolio as well as the realization of investments.
